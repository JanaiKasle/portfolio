My Projects

Real House Network

A web application designed to facilitate real estate transactions, connecting buyers and sellers without the need for intermediaries. It allows users to search and browse available rental properties based on their preferences, view property details, images, and contact landlords for inquiries.

Doctor Appointment Booking System

A website that enables patients to schedule appointments with medical professionals online, streamlining the appointment booking process and enhancing healthcare access. Users can choose preferred doctors, available time slots, and receive confirmation notifications, ensuring efficient healthcare management.

ASD Detection from Facial Images

Successfully trained a CNN model(MobileNet) to detect Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) using facial image data. With a test accuracy of 89.58% after 32 epochs, the model performed admirably and showed encouraging results in differentiating between people with and without ASD.

Comparative Study of Deep Neural Language Models for Text Generation

This project involves training Deep Neural language models such as CNN, RNN, LSTM, and GRU on the Shakespeare Dataset to assess accuracy and loss. It aims to compare the effectiveness of these models in generating text by considering factors.

Flight Fare Prediction

I developed a Flight Fare Prediction model using Random Forest Regressor to predict flight prices based on factors like stops, journey date, airline, and more. After training and fine-tuning the model through hyperparameter tuning, I deployed it using a Flask application on localhost for real-time predictions.
